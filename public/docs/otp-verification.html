How OTP Verification Works

The Visitor Management System uses email-based One-Time Password (OTP) verification as an additional security mechanism. This two-factor authentication process ensures that only authorized administrators and security personnel gain access to protected dashboard resources. Follow the steps below to complete OTP verification successfully.

  • Enter your registered login credentials and submit the authentication request through the secure login portal.
  • A unique One-Time Password (OTP) is automatically generated and delivered to your registered email address.
  • Open your email inbox and carefully locate the latest OTP message sent by the Visitor Management System.
  • Enter the received OTP exactly as displayed. OTP codes are case-sensitive and time-bound.
  • OTP codes remain valid only for a limited duration. Expired OTPs require a fresh login attempt.
  • Upon successful verification, a secure authenticated session is generated automatically.
  • Users are redirected to their respective dashboards after successful OTP validation.
  • Never share OTP codes with anyone. System administrators will never ask for your OTP.
