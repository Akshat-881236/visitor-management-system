Administrator Login Help

This guide explains the secure login workflow for administrators accessing the Visitor Management System.

Administrator Authentication Process

The Visitor Management System follows a secure two-tier authentication mechanism for administrator access. Every administrator must provide valid credentials followed by OTP verification before accessing dashboard resources. This process prevents unauthorized access and strengthens organizational security. Follow the instructions below carefully to complete the login procedure successfully.

  • Open the Administrator Portal from the homepage and navigate to the secure login page.
  • Enter the registered administrator email address exactly as configured by the system administrator.
  • Provide the correct administrator password. Passwords are case-sensitive and must be entered carefully.
  • After successful credential validation, a One-Time Password (OTP) is automatically delivered to the registered administrator email.
  • Enter the received OTP within the allowed validity duration. Expired OTPs require a fresh login attempt.
  • Upon successful OTP verification, a secure session is generated and the administrator is redirected to the dashboard.
  • Never share administrator credentials or OTP codes with any unauthorized individual.
  • Always logout after completing administrative tasks, especially when using shared devices.
